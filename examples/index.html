Compound design tokens

Design tokens are a way to define and manage design-related variables such as colors, typography, and spacing in a consistent and reusable manner. They can be used to define the visual design language of a product across different platforms, such as web, mobile, and print. They can be stored in a central location and be easily updated and shared across teams, allowing for consistent design across different platforms.
